Marketing Materials Filter By Format

Finding the right marketing materials is easy with our dynamic filtering system.

How to Use:

  1. Select a Format – First, select one format from the list of topics provided by selecting the dropdown icon. (Ads, Literature, etc.)
  2. Pick a Topic – When you click on the drop-down icon, you will be able to select the Topic of each asset. (e.g., Code (Laws in general), Hospitality, Pool). Make sure to hit the checkmark when it appears to filter the library.

    Note: You have to hover over a topic and the checkmark will appear (as shown in the red circle above). Click the checkmark to apply the filter.

  3. Browse & Grab What You Need – Scroll through the filtered docs to view, download, or share.
